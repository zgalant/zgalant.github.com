Lots of Books

Apr 9th, 2013

It turns out I spend a lot of time in the car. Over the past few weeks since I started listening to audiobooks, I’ve completed quite a few.

Here’s a list of them with a brief thought.

Tribal Leadership

Blog post here

The Innovator’s Dilemma

Love the idea of the disruptive innovation. Hate how the word disrupt has taken a life of its own that’s so far from this meaning.

The 4-Hour Workweek

Tim Ferriss is a bit ridiculous and pretty arrogant, but I liked a lot of the tips. The parts I liked most were the ones talking about outsourcing and working from home/anywhere – I’m in Tahoe writing this :)

The No Asshole Rule

Pretty obvious stuff here. Don’t be an asshole, don’t hire any assholes, but if you have one around, it can be a great bonding experience for the rest of the people.

How to Win Friends and Influence People

Lots of simple things, but you begin to notice them a lot.

Getting to Yes: Negotiating Agreement Without Giving In

Interesting thoughts on negotiation. It’s crazy how much of life is negotiable.
